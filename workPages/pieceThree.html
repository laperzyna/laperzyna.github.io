VISUAL UNSEEN ENVIRONMENTAL IMPACTS

Driven by a deep-rooted passion for marine life and the pressing challenge they face due to microplastics, this design topic serves as my call for awareness.

This piece aims to guide the viewer on a narrative journey: starting with the text that underscores the issue, transitioning to the careless disposal of trash from both cargo ships, and culminating in the imagery of turtles interacting with the plastic remnants. A particular challenge I faced was to seamlessly meld two perspectives â€“ the scene above and beneath the waves. Through many trials, I'm proud to have overcome this challenge.
