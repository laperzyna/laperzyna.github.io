ABOUT ME

Fourth year Computer Science and Design student at the University of San Francisco. I have a strong passion for tech and design and always try to find anyway to combine them in my day-to-day work. I discovered my love of art very early in my life, taking inspiration from the world and people around me, and direction of my life. I discovered my passion for tech and computer science in high school through an AP Computer Science class. I was taken with the ability to create art, applications, websites, etc. through code and knew this was a passion I wanted to pursue.
